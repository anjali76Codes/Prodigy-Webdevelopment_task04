Project

Mini Project

Food Donation App

In group of four we are developed a food donation app as a mini project. When users first visit our app, they need to register by providing their email and password. Once they have successfully submitted their account information, it will be secured. The app has two main sections: the donor section and the receiver section. In the donor section, anyone can add details about the food they want to donate, such as its name, type, quality, and other relevant information. On the other hand, the receiver section is exclusive to NGOs or charities who must undergo verification by the admin. Only after admin verification can receivers book donated foods, ensuring that fraudulent activities are minimized. Additionally, the app features a chat system, allowing users to freely ask any questions related to the app. We have implemented Firebase authentication to enhance the security of user accounts.
